About

Our website asks for two things: the visitor’s favorite animal and a picture of the visitor’s face. Using AI/ML libraries, the backend program finds the visitor’s emotions. An image of the visitor’s favorite animal with the same emotions is subsequently generated, along with general information about it.

Our mission with this project was to raise awareness of the vast animal kingdom; to see a glimpse of their environments and diverse appearances. Visitors are presented with their favorite animal(s) experiencing similar emotions, as well as further information about the species. It not only strengthens the desire to learn more about them but allows the visitor to see possible similarities and connections. According to a research paper titled “Number of Threatened Species is Rising”, the number of threatened or endangered animals (mammals, insects, mollusks, fish, reptiles, etc.) has risen rapidly over the past 15 years (Katharina Buchholz). It is our duty to ensure that these threatened species (or any wild species) avoid extinction. This can be accomplished through implementing more ecologically friendly behaviors, supporting conservation initiatives, and minimizing our environmental effects. We need to protect against the ever-increasing dangers that wild animals face in the presence of modern societal changes.
